Rules For Racing Showdown

1. Obey all rules.
2. WINS will be defined as the number of best place finishes as compared to the other listed drivers.
    a.  These WINS will be maintained and calculated by the Keeper of the WINS (Devo).
    b.  WINS will only be taken from races where points are awarded.  No Duels or All Stars or Showdowns, etc.
3. All 'donations' to the SPONSOR with the most WINS will take place at the next camping trip immediately following the end of the race season.
  The SPONSOR with the most wins must be physically at the camp site, during the next camping trip, by midnight Saturday following the end of the race season to collect his 'donations'.
  Any ties for the most WINS among two or more SPONSORS will be settled between those SPONSORS as well as the decision on who picks in what order after other non-winning SPONSORS have picked.
4. Each SPONSOR must be present to select his DRIVER on the Saturday of the camping trip, and the selection must be done as a group containing all SPONSORS.
5. Selection Order for picking drivers is as follows :
      a. The SPONSOR with the most wins from the previous race season will pick last.  See rule 3 in the event of a tie.
      b. The remaining SPONSORS will randomly select cards and DRIVERS will be picked based upon ascending order of the cards  pulled.  Ace is a one.
6. If a DRIVER is removed from the race season, at any time during the season, for any reason, that SPONSOR will be allowed to keep the same car and accept the substitute driver.
      a. If the SPONSOR chooses not to keep the same car, he is still required to make the agreed upon 'donation' to the SPONSOR with the most WINS.
7. The amount of the 'donation' will be verbally agreed upon at each camping trip.
8. Any changes and/or additions to the rules must be verbally stated before the selection of DRIVERS and agreed upon by a majority of the SPONSORS.
9. Any complaints must be submitted in writing in triplicate before the first full moon during a leap year to the Whining Committee.
      a. All complaints will be scrutinized, sanitized, vandalized, and filed in the special Enron cabinet (the shredder).

NOTE: Rules were modified, accepted, and updated by majority vote during the 2016 Board of Directors meeting.
