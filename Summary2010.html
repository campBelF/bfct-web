Q&A

"When are yall going to end this ridiculous camping trip?"
Uncle Devo is here to provide an answer.  After much planning, all campers have agreed to end the camping trip in the year 2069,
and implement a new and improved camping trip starting in February 2070 which will continue forever.  To summarize: the camping trip
does not end!  Ever!  It is its own entity.  We have no control over the camping trip.  We must attend the camping trip or the world will
cease to exist as we know it.  If it were not for the annual campers being so dedicated, you would be living in utter chaos and total disarray.
Do not ever question the camping trip.  It can not be stopped!  It cannot be reasoned with!
